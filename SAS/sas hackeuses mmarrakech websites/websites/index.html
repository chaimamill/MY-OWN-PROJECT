About Us

About simplon.co

Simplon.co is a network of social digital factories that offer free, intensive courses in digital professions in France and abroad. Simplon maghreb has opened in Youssoufia, Morocco, in October 2018 its first “YouCode” training center and in the next year simplon opened the second youcode training center in 2019, with 110 trainees in Web & Mobile Development.The first cohort has been launched in partnership with the OCP (Office Chérifien de Phosphates). As a result of this success, two new cohorts of 110 trainees each started their training programs in October 2019 respectively in Youssoufia and Safi. Simplon has further expanded its network of digital training centers with 60 new trainees in Web & Mobile Development scheduled to begin soon. SoliCode is created in partnership with the Mohammed V Foundation for Solidarity.
